Spacia

Spacia is a visual project management tool. It's still under heavy development, but is currently live and operational.

I designed, implemented, and deployed the entire project over the course of a year. It's a VueJS app deployed as horizontally scalable Docker containers on a Nomad cluster using fully-automated Ansible scripts. Spacia is running on DigitalOcean Droplets, and makes use of DigitalOcean's managed Postgres and Spaces offerings.
