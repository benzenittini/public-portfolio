Spacia

Spacia is a visual project management tool. It uses a mindmap as its foundation and builds the more conventional task tracking features on top, making it ideal for all of us visually-organized people. It's still under development, but is live and operational.

I designed, implemented, and deployed the entire project over the course of a year. It's a VueJS app deployed as horizontally scalable Docker containers on a Nomad cluster via Ansible scripts. Spacia is running on DigitalOcean Droplets, and makes use of DigitalOcean's managed Postgres and Spaces offerings.
